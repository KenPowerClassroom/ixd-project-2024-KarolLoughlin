Introduction

Hello. Welcome to my Interaction Design Project. I will be focusing on a design made to gameify a learning app. Our aim is to make the learning experience more engaging and interactive for our users.

Gamification involes applying game design principles to non-game contexts, such as education. The objective is to increase user motivation, engagement and enjoyment.

This project involves various phases, including a competitive analysis, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. The competitive analysis will help me identify strengths and weaknesses in the existing market, and highlight areas we can improve.

Wireframing and prototyping allowed us to design and develop the interface and user experience of the app, ensuring a much smoother and enjoyable learning process.

Finally, usability testing was conducted to gather feedback from users on the app's usability and functionality. The feedback will be used to refine and improve the app, ensuring that the user's needs are met.

Overall, our aim is to create a learning app that is not only effective but enjoyable, ultimately improving the user's learning outcomes.
